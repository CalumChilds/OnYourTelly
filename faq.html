FAQ

What is this website?

On Your Telly is a website that allows you to search for TV shows and see what's on TV where you are.

Who created this and why?

I (Calum Childs) originally created On Your Telly as coursework for my Computing degree that I'm doing at the University of Suffolk.

Where do you get the data from?

All provided data is from the TV Maze API.
